Bitcoin Eyes $100k as ETF Flows Rise

Bitcoin surged following a week of strong ETF inflows, pushing BTC toward a critical psychological level. Traders are split on whether a clean break above $100k can happen before year-end.

Options markets show increased open interest around the $100k strike, while macro traders point to rate expectations as a key variable for risk assets.

The next CPI print and upcoming central bank commentary are expected to influence sentiment in the near term.
